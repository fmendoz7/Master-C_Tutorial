COMMON COMMON
    - Native UNIX form of C
    - Also called "Bell Labs C" or "K & R C" or "Classic C"

ANSI C
    - Defined by American National Standards Institute
    - Will inevitably overtake and replace common COMMON

    - Contains several improvements over old common C such as:
        > Function Declarations more like Pascal procedures
        > Different Grammar
        > Supported by SUN workstation compilers
